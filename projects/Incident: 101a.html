Incident: 101a Project

To make this project, I had to use basic HTML and CSS to make a webpage for a movie. Me and my partner made a planning page to organize our thoughts and to see who should do what to make our story. Our story starts with a lone wanderer stalking around his broken city, drenched by his own guilt.

Our main problem while working on this project was figuring out who should do what first in order to keep our project organized and look nice. We talked about how we could organize what we had to do into steps on what we should do first then last. BY doing this, we managed to make our project neat, nice, and organized. We both learned that by focusing on certain points first, then work on other points next, we can work a lot more efficient.
